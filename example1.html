Toxic Activity Spilling Out

Tumblr has been a place for expression and sharing but that means for negative behavior as well. There is a huge community of eating disorders especially anorexia.





Does This Activity Turn Up In Other Corners of the Internet?

This community around EDs has become so toxic that Tumblr started changing the platform in the recent years. However, people just started using different words and tags. But since what we search for ends up in in front of us at every turn of the internet, does the algorithm feed our negative behavior as well. As someone that has consumed the content of this toxic Tumblr community, I would often get ads about Diet Pills or products of Diet Culture even when I didn't search for them. I felt as if the internet fed into whatever behavior it could latch onto even negative ones...
